About Us

Total Home Services Cleaning is a family owned, private cleaning company serving Manatee Co. for over 25 years. We have experience with all types of residential, commercial, and construction cleaning and lots of it. Our highly trained and efficient staff will deliver effective cleaning for your home, office, or resort with little to no supervision, but our managing partners won’t ever take a backseat role when it comes to caring for your property. The owners and managers follow each job very closely to ensure clients who choose us are well cared for, and get the biggest bang for their buck.
